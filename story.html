OUR FOOD

Style

Flour House cuisine is simple and healthful because it reflects the way real Italians have cooked and eaten for generations.

Hand Crafted

In the hands of someone who knows how to use them, even the humblest of ingredients like flour and water can be transformed into delicious pizzas and pastas.

Unique

Combine that expertise with the abundant produce of the Central Coast and the only authentic, imported Stefano Ferrara wood-burning pizza oven between Los Angeles and San Francisco, and you have an establishment like no other in San Luis Obispo.

OUR STORY

Creators

Flour House is the project of a husband-and-wife team - one Italian by birth and the other by blook - who bring Italy's modern aesthetic and obsession wiwth quality to the Central Coast restaurant scene.

Influence

Their shared love for Italian culture and cuisine compelled them to open Flour House, a modern eatery offering house-made pasta, aperitivi, and specializing in pizza napoletana, a heritage style so precious that it's guarded by Italian law: simple, light, fresh, flavorful, and delicious.
